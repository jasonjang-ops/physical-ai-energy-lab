Core Research Areas / 핵심 연구 분야

Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)

Developing deep learning models constrained by thermodynamics for accurate battery SOH and SOC estimation.

Digital Twin for Saemangeum

Creating virtual replicas of the 2.1GW PV/Wind infrastructure for real-time ESS optimization and fault diagnosis.

Intelligent Control & Integration

Bridging Physical AI algorithms with industrial BMS through robust, embedded edge computing solutions.
