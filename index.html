Research on Intelligent Control of Energy Storage Systems Based on Physical Artificial Intelligence and Integration with Education and Industry

In the Saemangeum Intelligent Energy Cluster, we have deeply integrated the laws of physics with deep learning.
Devote to developing the next-generation efficient and secure energy storage system (ESS) control algorithms and smart grid technologies.

Core Research Area / Core Research

Physical AI & PINNs

Introduce thermodynamic and dynamic physical constraints into neural networks to achieve ultra-high-precision prediction of battery health status (SOH).

ESS intelligent control

A multi-objective optimization control strategy based on reinforcement learning is employed to enhance the response speed of large-scale energy storage systems in complex power grid environments.

Saemangeum Industry-Education Integration

Leveraging the measured data from the 2.1GW Saemangeum Renewable Energy Base, a closed-loop verification platform spanning from theoretical concepts to industrial applications will be established.

Saemangeum Experimental Base / Saemangeum Site

As the largest renewable energy cluster in South Korea, Saemangeum provides us with unparalleled on-site data support for our research. The laboratory, through deep academic-industrial cooperation with Kunsan National University in South Korea, directly participates in the energy storage and peak shaving experiments for the integration of large-scale photovoltaic and wind power.
