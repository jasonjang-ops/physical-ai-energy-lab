Research / 연구 분야

Intelligent Control of ESS using Physical AI

물리적 인공지능 기반 ESS 지능형 제어

Physical AI integrates physics-based modeling with machine learning to create robust control systems for energy infrastructure such as Energy Storage Systems (ESS).

Research Component Description
Battery State Estimation AI estimation of SoC, SoH, and degradation
Predictive Energy Dispatch Optimization of charge/discharge scheduling
Grid Interaction Adaptive control for renewable intermittency
Thermal Safety Modeling Physics-informed safety modeling

Saemangeum Case Study / 새만금 산학연계 사례

The Saemangeum Renewable Energy Cluster is one of the largest renewable infrastructure projects in Asia, designed to integrate solar, wind, and large-scale ESS deployment.

Sector Role
Universities AI algorithms & modeling
Research Institutes Grid simulation & safety testing
Industry ESS production and deployment
Government Infrastructure and policy support
Physical AI driven ESS control enables stable renewable integration and scalable energy management.
